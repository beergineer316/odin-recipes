Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Pasta Sauce
  • ricotta cheese
  • lasagna noodles
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Add the sauce
  4. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta
  5. Repeat until sauce is gone
  6. top with mozzarella
  7. bake until brown and crispy on top, about 45 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
