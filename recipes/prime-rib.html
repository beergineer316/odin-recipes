Prime Rib Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4lb Prime Rib Roast
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Black Pepper
  • Herbs de Provence
  • Kosher Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees
  2. Combine butter and herbs in bowl and cover roast
  3. Roast in oven for 20 minutes
  4. Turn oven off and let sit for 2 hours
  5. Remove roast from oven, slice and serve
  6. Enjoy!
