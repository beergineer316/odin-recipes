Ice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2.25C Milk
  • 1C Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 3/4C White Sugar
  • 2tsp Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Gather All Ingredients
  2. Combine milk, cream and sugar in sauce pan over medium heat
  3. Transfer to bowl and stir in vanilla
  4. Chill for at least 2 hours
  5. Pour into Ice Cream machine and run for 20-25 minutes
  6. Freeze and Enjoy!
